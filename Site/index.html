We work together

With our parteners we aim at ensuring a bright future for those at disadvantage

In the rugged Eastern region of the Democratic Republic of Congo, amidst the long lasting conflicts , wars and catastrophies where life can be tough, LightAndSalt offers a haven of hope. We're a team of dedicated individuals working alongside communities to build a brighter future for children and families.

Hunger? We fight it. We provide nutritious meals in schools and communities, ensuring stomachs are full and minds can focus on learning.

Sickness? We combat it. We partner with healthcare professionals to deliver essential medical care, preventing illness and building healthy bodies.

Education? We believe in it. We build safe and empowering learning environments, equipping children with the tools and knowledge to chase their dreams.

But we can't do it alone. LightAndSalt thrives on the power of partnership. Join us as a volunteer, donor, or advocate. Together, we can shine brighter and change lives in Eastern DRC.

Here, amidst the challenges, we work together. We empower. We nurture. We build. We are LightAndSalt. Be the light. Be the salt.
